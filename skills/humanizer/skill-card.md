## Description: <br>
Remove signs of AI-generated writing from text and rewrite passages so they sound more natural while preserving the original meaning. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>

## Use Case: <br>
Writers, editors, and reviewers use this skill to identify common AI-writing patterns and revise text for a more natural voice. It can work on supplied text or local documents, with the user reviewing edits before saving. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: The skill may be misused to hide AI assistance where disclosure is expected. <br>
Mitigation: Use it as a writing and editing aid only, and follow academic, workplace, legal, publishing, or other AI-disclosure rules. <br>
Risk: The skill can rewrite local documents when file editing tools are available. <br>
Mitigation: Review proposed changes before saving or publishing edited text. <br>
